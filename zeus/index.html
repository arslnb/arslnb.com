Zeus

An agent that works in the background while you do your thing.

Runs on your Mac. No Mac Mini required. Zeus handles your inbox, calendar, code, research, and messaging — quietly, safely, with approval before anything that matters. Supports skills, tools, and multi-step workflows. Free and local.

Capabilities

  • Inbox & Calendar

    Reads, drafts, and sends email and iMessages. Manages Apple and Google Calendar.

  • Code & Shell

    Runs commands in a sandboxed shell. Reads, edits, and searches your codebase.

  • Research & Web

    Browses the web, fetches pages, and runs background research autonomously.

  • Skills & Tools

    Extensible with custom skills and MCP tool servers. Compose multi-step workflows.

  • Approval Gates

    Nothing leaves your machine without your say-so. Every sensitive action requires approval.

  • Memory

    Learns your preferences, contacts, and context over time. Gets better the more you use it.
